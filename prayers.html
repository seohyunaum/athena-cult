Psalms for Athena
blessings: 1
Mighty Athena, with wisdom's grace,
Guide my steps in this earthly race.
Grant me strength to face each trial,
With your wisdom, I'll walk in style.
blessings: 2
Goddess Athena, fair and wise,
Hear my pleas, for in you, my hope lies.
Grant me wisdom, in my actions and speech,
So I may honor you, as I beseech.
blessings: 3
Athena, goddess of wisdom and grace,
In your favor, I find my rightful place.
Grant me knowledge and clarity of mind,
With your blessings, may I always be aligned.
blessings: 4
Athena, whose wisdom knows no bounds,
In your presence, my spirit resounds.
Guide me on my journey, protect and adore,
With your favor, I'll seek wisdom even more.
motivation: 5
Athena, with your intellect so keen,
Inspire in me a drive that's never seen.
Motivate me to seek wisdom's embrace,
To emulate you in wisdom and grace.
motivation: 6
Athena, protector of the wise and just,
In your guide, I place my trust.
Bless me with the will to constantly grow,
To walk the path of wisdom as you bestow.
motivation: 7
Athena, with your owl as a guide,
Nurture my spirit, let it not subside.
Guide my thoughts and actions, I pray,
To be more like you in every way.
motivation: 8
Athena, patron of artful grace,
Teach me to excel in every place.
With creativity, my heart you fire,
To be like you, my ultimate desire.
Ode to Athena
In ancient halls where wisdom's light does gleam,
I sing to Athena, goddess of the dream.
With heart ablaze, I raise my voice on high,
To praise the one whose wisdom fills the sky.

Athena, with your intellect so keen,
In you, the source of knowledge, I have seen.
In reverence, I stand before your grace,
To honor you in this sacred space.

Oh, Athena, goddess, fair and wise,
With every challenge, you've been my prize.
Your favor, like a beacon in the night,
Guides my path with your eternal light.

You've nurtured in my soul a deep desire,
To kindle wisdom's ever-burning fire.
With every step, with every thought I ponder,
Your wisdom leads me to knowledge yonder.

In the quiet moments of reflection deep,
It's your presence that I often keep.
Through trials and triumphs, you've stood by my side,
My constant muse, my intellectual guide.

From ancient Greece to this modern day,
Your wisdom's gifts continue to light my way.
In your name, I strive to reach my best,
To honor you above all the rest.

With every book I read, with every word I write,
I aim to mirror your brilliant light.
To be like you, Athena, is my call,
For in your wisdom, I find my all in all.

With every question that I dare to ask,
I seek the answers in your sacred task.
In your name, I'll journey ever higher,
To quench my thirst for intellectual fire.

So, Athena, hear my heartfelt plea,
I'll praise you to be the center of it all, you see.
For your favor, I am truly blessed,
In your wisdom, I find eternal rest.

In the realm of knowledge, you're my muse,
With your guidance, I'll never lose.
To Athena, goddess, fair and wise,
In endless praise, my spirit flies.
